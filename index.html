THE ESTATE

A Venue Like No Other

Welcome to D&G Garden

Most wedding venues offer four walls and a roof. We offer a true domain. When you step onto the property, you are greeted by an expansive, open-air environment surrounded by lush, manicured gardens that create a natural, breathtaking backdrop for your special day.

The estate features expansive greenery throughout, with carefully curated gardens that provide endless backdrops for photography and intimate moments. Every corner of D&G Garden has been designed to celebrate the beauty of nature while offering the pinnacle of luxury.

The Gardens

Our expansive gardens are the heart of the estate. Rolling lawns, tropical flora, and hidden pathways create an enchanting atmosphere for your celebration. Whether you dream of an outdoor ceremony under a floral arch or a cocktail hour amidst the greenery, the gardens provide endless possibilities.

The Grand Swimming Pool

The crown jewel of the estate is our massive swimming pool — a rare feature for wedding venues. This isn't just a scenic water feature; it is an active part of your celebration. Families can arrive early, guests can cool off, and children can swim and enjoy themselves before, during, and after the main wedding events.

The pool area is designed for both elegance and enjoyment, with luxurious loungers, shaded areas, and ambient evening lighting that transforms the space into a magical setting for sunset receptions.
