Your Hustle Becomes Ours

The Ultimate Mauritian Wedding Experience

Planning a wedding is often described as one of the most stressful experiences in a couple's life. The endless checklists, the coordinating of a dozen different vendors, and the constant worry that something might slip through the cracks can easily overshadow the joy of the occasion. But it doesn't have to be this way.

There is a venue in Mauritius fundamentally changing how weddings are hosted. We aren't just renting out a room; we are providing a complete, stress‑free masterpiece. From the moment you book with us, our core philosophy takes over: Your hustle is our hustle. Your problem is our problem. Here is why D&G Garden stands entirely unmatched in the Mauritian wedding industry.

Welcome to the Domain

Most wedding venues offer four walls and a roof. We offer a true domain. When you step onto the property, you are greeted by an expansive, open‑air environment surrounded by beautiful, lush trees that create a natural, breathtaking backdrop for your special day.

But the true crown jewel of the estate—and something incredibly rare for wedding venues—is our massive swimming pool. This isn't just a scenic water feature; it is an active part of your celebration. Families can arrive early, and children can safely swim and enjoy themselves before, during, and even after the main wedding events. It provides an extra layer of entertainment and luxury that most other places simply cannot offer, turning a standard wedding day into a true family getaway.

The estate spans over two hectares of manicured gardens, with the grand pool as its shimmering centrepiece. Whether you dream of an intimate gathering or a grand celebration of 300 guests, the space adapts to your vision.
